Rules and Regulations

1. Participating teams will have to prepare a report of their Presentation with required graphic details, in less than 1000 words, and mail it to the address of events@pantheon18.in

2. The selected teams will be sent mails regarding their selection and will be informed about the further rounds.

3. The teams will be given 15 minutes to present their research paper with a supplementary PowerPoint presentation.

4. The selected teams have to submit at least five hard copies of their research to the esteemed panel who will be scrutinizing their research.

5. There will be an additional viva session of every team at the end of their presentations.

6. The teams will be judged on their content, level of research, presentation skills and viva performances.

7. The dress code will be strictly formal wears.

8. Plagiarism is strictly prohibited.

9. The decision of the Judging Panel will be final and binding.

Team Size: 4-5
