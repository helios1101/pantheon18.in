ABOUT PANTHEON

About Us


Pantheon, one of the biggest technical fests of Eastern India, is an intricately strung assortment of technological marvels blended with a taste of youthful exuberance. Our campus has been frequented by professors from IITs, IIMs and other elite institutes during Pantheon. We also had guest lectures from developers in Silicon Valley, University of Pretoria as well as the internationally acclaimed Former Head of the Astrophysics Department at Bhaba Atomic Research Centre, Dr. Abhas Mitra. The fact that the previous edition of Pantheon had a footfall of more than 1000 students and educators, with more than 50 day events, guest lectures and night events, stands a testament to how dedicated the college has been to the cause of education.

Our Mascot


The ability to build our own securities is what we wanted all along. Keeping in sync with the theme of Pantheon'18, ‘Techstones-Then.Now.Next’, we have our official mascot ‘Raksha’. It is a saviour, representing the eternal Shakti in its purest but technical form. Infusing the best of the technologies, this futuristic robot comes along as someone so invulnerable that people would look up to it for their insecurities.
