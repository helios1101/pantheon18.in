ABOUT PANTHEON

About Us


Pantheon, one of the biggest technical fests of Eastern India, in its truest sense, is an intricately strung assortment of technological marvels blended with a unique taste of youthful exuberance.  Set in the verdant campus of Birla Institute of Technology, Mesra, Pantheon boasts of an exquisite set of events and workshops. Our campus has been frequented by renowned professors from IITs, IIMs and other elite institutes during Pantheon. In its previous edition, we had tried to transcend national boundaries by having guest lectures from developers in Silicon Valley,  University of Pretoria as well as the internationally acclaimed Former Head of the Astrophysics Department at Bhaba Atomic Research Centre, Dr. Abhas Mitra. We have always strived to instill a sense of awareness for the modern marvels mankind has discovered during the course of time. The fact that the previous edition of Pantheon had a footfall of more than 1000 students and educators, with more than 50 day events, guest lectures and night events, stands a testament to how dedicated the college has been to the cause. On that note, the much awaited technical extravaganza is waiting to be unfurled yet again, bigger and better than it has ever been.

Our Mascot


The ability to build our own securities is what we wanted all along. Keeping in sync with the theme of Pantheon'18, ‘Techstones-Then.Now.Next’, we have our official mascot ‘Raksha’. It is a saviour, representing the eternal Shakti in its purest but technical form. Infusing the best of the technologies, this futuristic robot comes along as someone so invulnerable that people would look up to it for their insecurities.
