Event Description

Eureka, the flagship event of Pantheon'18 is an opportunity for the smartest and quirkiest teenagers to bring out the explorer in themselves. For the preliminary round, the basic idea of the project is to be mailed along with the relevant pictures. The best entries get a chance to feature their models for the final exhibition.

Where innovation meets vision

Rules and Regulations

1. Participants will have to prepare a report of their presentation with required photographs as a proof of their project progress, in less than 1000 words, and mail it to the address of events@pantheon18.in before October.

2. The selected teams will be sent mails regarding their selection and will be informed about the further rounds.

3. The selected teams will have to be present during the entire exhibition and will have to explain their project to the technical enthusiasts present.

4. The teams will also be explaining their project to the judging panel who will scrutinize their project.

5. The teams will be given a total time of 10 minutes to explain their project.

6. The teams will be marked on their content, level of research, presentation skills and most importantly crowd interaction skills.

7. The decision of the judging panel will be final and binding.

Team Size: 4-5

Theme: Innovation + Vision = Innovision

Droid Trooper

Event Description

The contestants have to make a manual robot which can be wired or wireless and the bot has to traverse a terrain consisting of different zones. While traversing it has to perform certain task (which will be mentioned in the rulebook) by any suitable mechanism and reach the finish point. Points will be awarded for crossing terrains and performing the task.

Overcoming obstacles with Technology

Rules and Regulations

1. There will be certain time limit (mentioned in the rulebook) to complete the challenge after which there will be penalty.

2. Top 4 teams with maximum points will qualify for the finals.

3. A team should contain only 4 members.

4. 2 attempts will be given to a team and the best of 2 attempts will be recorded.

5. The dimensions of the bot will be mentioned in rulebook.

6. Details about points and penalties will be given in the problem statement along with the different views of the game field.

7. Basic components such as motors, wheels, switches, motor clamps etc. will provided by ROBOLUTION at nominal rates or contestants can buy their own components.

Team Size: 2-4
