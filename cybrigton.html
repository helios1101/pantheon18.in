Cyber-BridgeTon

Event Description

Cost, time and placement constraints are all considered in this design marathon. Ingenuity and speed are your friends while tardiness will lead you astray when making simulation bridges. This is a 2 round event, with a preliminary round and a final round. This event requires participants to design a bridge, which can withstand both structural and environmental challenges. The fundamental knowledge of physics and the creativity of theteams will be tested in a cohesive manner in this event. The team with the best design will be adjudged as the winners.
