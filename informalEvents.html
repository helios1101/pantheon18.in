Event Name : Mediacre

Team Size: 2-3

Event Description :

Round 1: The participants will be given one word at a time. They will have 60 seconds to write as many small words as possible from that one big word within those 60 seconds. The one with the highest word count of legitimate words (with letters from that one big word) wins.

Round 2: The round exemplifies the classical game of ‘Pictureka’. One of the teammates will be given a picture to draw and the other participant will have to guess it. The teams to guess in a lesser time will move on to the final round.

Round 3: The round tests the creativity of the participants as they will have to suggest divergent titles through a series of video clips.
